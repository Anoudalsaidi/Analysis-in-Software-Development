# Banking System - Simple Product Backlog

## User Stories

1. **Create Account**  
   - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can register, receive confirmation, and activate account.  

2. **Login Securely**  
   - **Story Points:** 3 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User logs in with credentials; 2FA optional.  

3. **Update Personal Details**  
   - **Story Points:** 3 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** Medium  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can update contact info and changes are saved.  

4. **Transfer Between Accounts**  
   - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User selects accounts and amount; transfer succeeds.  

5. **Transfer to Other Users**  
   - **Story Points:** 8 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User selects recipient; transfer succeeds or fails with message.  

6. **Schedule Recurring Transfers**  
   - **Story Points:** 8 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** Medium  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User sets frequency; notifications sent before transfer.  

7. **Apply for Loan**  
   - **Story Points:** 13 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User enters loan details; eligibility checked; status updated.  

8. **View Loan Balance & Schedule**  
   - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** Shows balance, due dates, and payment history.  

9. **View Account Statements**  
   - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** High  
   - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can view/download statements.  

10. **Transaction Notifications**  
    - **Story Points:** 3 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** User receives SMS/email for transactions.  

11. **Reset Password**  
    - **Story Points:** 3 | **Priority:** High | **Business Value:** High  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can reset password securely via email.  

12. **Enable 2FA**  
    - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** High  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can enable/disable 2FA; OTP sent on login.  

13. **Contact Support via Chat**  
    - **Story Points:** 8 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** Medium  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** Chat is real-time and responses are shown immediately.  

14. **Submit Complaint/Feedback**  
    - **Story Points:** 5 | **Priority:** Medium | **Business Value:** Medium  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can submit complaint; status tracked by bank.  

15. **View FAQs**  
    - **Story Points:** 3 | **Priority:** Low | **Business Value:** Medium  
    - **Acceptance Criteria:** User can browse/search FAQs.
